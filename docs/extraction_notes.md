
# Extraction Note: 40 Gy/10 fx Palliative Lung Study

## Study Design & Population
- **Design**: Retrospective cohort, multicenter (QEH, UCH), Jan 2017–Dec 2022.
- **N**: 62 patients.
- **Age**: Median 72.6y.
- **Performance Status**: Poor PS predominates (ECOG ≥2 in 83.4% of patients).
- **Inclusion**: NSCLC, treated with 40Gy/10fx.
- **Exclusion**: No cross-sectional imaging for response assessment.

## Regimen & Technique
- **Dose**: 40 Gy in 10 fractions (daily).
- **Technique**: VMAT (87.1%), 3DCRT (12.9%).
- **Rationale for 40/10**:
  - Patients unsuitable for SBRT (85.4%) due to large size (>5cm, 54.8%) or ultracentral location (30.6%).
  - Aim: Higher BED than 30/10 for better local control while keeping toxicity acceptable.
  - Median PTV: 168.8 cm³.

## Outcomes
- **Local Control (LC)**:
  - 1-year: 96.7%
  - 3-year: 76.9%
- **Response Rate (ORR)**: 69.3% (RECIST 1.1).
- **Overall Survival (OS)**:
  - 1-year: 88.6%
  - 3-year: 61.4%

## Toxicity
- **Esophagitis**:
  - G1-2: 8.1%
  - G3: 1 case (1.6%) - associated with esophageal overlap (Dmax 42.4 Gy).
- **Pneumonitis**:
  - G1-2: 46.7% (radiological mainly, asymptomatic).
  - G≥3: 0%.

## BED/EQD2 Context (Table S4, α/β=10)
- **10 Gy / 1 fx**: BED10 20.0 Gy, EQD2 16.7 Gy
- **20 Gy / 5 fx**: BED10 28.0 Gy, EQD2 23.3 Gy
- **30 Gy / 10 fx**: BED10 39.0 Gy, EQD2 32.5 Gy
- **40 Gy / 10 fx**: BED10 56.0 Gy, EQD2 46.7 Gy

## Limitations
- Retrospective nature (adverse events may be underreported).
- Response assessment complexity (fibrosis vs tumor).
- Systemic therapy changes (29% had change) may confound control (addressed via sensitivity analysis).
